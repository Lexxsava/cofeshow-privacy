Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Cofeshow (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information Collection

Cofeshow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app works entirely on the user’s device and processes only the content you choose to use, such as photos, videos, and music from your personal library.

All content remains on your device unless you explicitly export or share it yourself.

2. User Content

Photos, videos, and audio selected by the user are used solely to create video slideshows.

Cofeshow does not upload, analyze, or share this content with any servers or third parties.

3. Tracking

Cofeshow does not track users.

  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No cross-app tracking
  • No third-party advertising SDKs

4. Third-Party Services

Cofeshow does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

5. Data Sharing

Since no personal data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Cofeshow does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact
