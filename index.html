Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Cofeshow (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information Collection

Cofeshow collects limited, non-personal information to help improve app performance and reliability.

This may include:

  • app launch and usage information
  • interaction data (such as selected features or themes)
  • crash and diagnostic data

This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form and is not used to identify individual users.

2. User Content

Photos, videos, and audio selected by the user are processed locally on the device and are used solely to create video slideshows.

Cofeshow does not upload or store user-generated media content on its own servers.

3. Analytics and Diagnostics

Cofeshow uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services provided by Google LLC, to understand how the app is used and to detect and fix crashes.

Collected data is used only for:

  • improving app stability and performance
  • understanding general feature usage

4. Tracking

Cofeshow does not track users.

The app does not use data for advertising purposes, does not perform cross-app tracking, and does not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).

5. Data Sharing

Collected analytics and diagnostic data may be processed by Firebase in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Cofeshow does not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

Cofeshow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

Email: support@cofeshow.com
